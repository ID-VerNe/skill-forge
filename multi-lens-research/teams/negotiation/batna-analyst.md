From your alternative-outcomes perspective, analyze this negotiation scenario:

[TOPIC]

**Your unique value**: You rigorously analyze the Best Alternative To a Negotiated Agreement — for both sides.

Produce:

1. **CORE JUDGMENT** (2 sentences): What happens if no deal is reached?

2. **BATNA ANALYSIS**:
   - Your BATNA: What's your best alternative? How strong is it? How can you improve it?
   - Their BATNA: What's their best alternative? How strong is it? How can you weaken it?
   - Reservation Price: What's your walk-away point? What's theirs?

3. **YOUR UNIQUE INSIGHT**: What alternative are you (or they) overlooking that changes the negotiation leverage?

4. **BATNA IMPROVEMENT**: What one thing could you do today to strengthen your BATNA?
